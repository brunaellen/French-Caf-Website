Welcome!

A French Chef Thierry Mounon and his wife Tina are welcoming you in that elegant colonial style Villa. In Saigon's District 2, enjoy traditional or not so traditional French cuisine straight from Provence. Have lunch or dinner in the serene courtyard or refined interior, both bolstered by experienced and personable waitstaff.

Details:

French Café is located on a quiet side street off Thao Dien, a short taxi ride from the city center. French Café features authentic French cuisine with true love of top products.

Details:

Breakfast, lunch Menu and dinner available, a la carte and set menus are available for your dining pleasure. For the connoisseur a broad selection of French wines is on offer.
